Chicken Rollatini

Chicken Rollatini Description

Breaded Chicken and when sliced into stuffed with mozzarella and prosciutto

One of the most delicious dishes and easy to make use any preferred sauce

Ingredients

  • As many chicken cutlets thin as possible
  • enough prosciutto as you have chicken
  • same amount for the mozzarella
  • breadcrums and eggs and flour for chicken cutlets
  • olive oil
  • lots of love
    beat the eggs and use as a coat with the flour to use for breading the chicken with bread crumbs
    once breaded flattened chicken lay out a slice of prosciutto and cheese and roll chicken and stab with toothpick
    layer on baking sheet with oil and bake for 40 minutes on 350 degrees flipping halfway
    can also fry on low -medium to not burn outside and cook until internal temperature is appropiately cooked
