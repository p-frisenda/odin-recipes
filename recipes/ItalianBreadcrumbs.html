Italian Breadcrumbs Description

A mix of italian seasonings with crushed bread flakes used for all types of dishes

Can be used for meatballs, chicken, braciole, sauce ect..

Ingredients

  • Stale loaf of bread or frozen this is because it makes it easier to chop into finer bits
  • 4 chopped cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups pecorino romano cheese
  • handful of washed dry parsley
  • blender
  • lots of love
    wash and chop seasonings
    chop bread with knive to fit in blender
    blend all ingreadients for several miniutes you dont want a paste however
    put in bag and use as needed for your future dishes to coat or mix in with
