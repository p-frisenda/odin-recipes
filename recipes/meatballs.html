Meatballs

Meatballs Description

A mix of the finest ground beef sourced locally ground and mixed with fresh seasonings and cheese

One of the most simplest dishes and delicious meals onces smothered in tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup pecorino romano cheese
  • handful of fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 large loaf of stale bread
  • garlic
  • lots of love
    chop ingredients by hand or in blender including meat
    once mixed roll with hands into tiny balls and place on baking sheet
    broil for 5 minutes on low or until outside is crisp
    toss meatballs in sauce on stove cooking on a simmer or low for several hours
